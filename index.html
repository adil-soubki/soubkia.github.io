I am a physics PhD student at Cambridge in the Institute of Astronomy. My research is broadly interested in theoretical physics and its intersection with machine learning and AI (but I'm new here). I am advised by Miles Cranmer.

Previously (March 2025), I completed a PhD in computer science at Stony Brook University where I was a member of the Institute for Advanced Computational Science (IACS). My research (unsurprisingly) involved NLP and machine learning. Owen Rambow believed in me first.
