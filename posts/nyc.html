none of this is in order. i might add some sort of an indicator for stuff that balls especially hard.

food

  • - minetta tavern is probably my favorite overall
  • - lillo cucina italiana
  • - pho bang - pho (obviously), bun cha hanoi, bahn mi
  • - keens - mutton with mint jelly, steaks are also good
  • - don't go to peter luger it's not worth it
  • - great ny noodle town - get the roast duck, bbq pork, pea shoots, and a coke
  • - hamburger america - get the oklahoma onion burger
  • - eileen's special cheesecake
  • - chuan tian xia
  • - it's not a secret but scarr's is the best ny slice
  • - decoy for peking duck
  • - ichiran for ramen; the macha beer is also kinda fun
  • - i have a bunch but these are some that come to mind. message me if you want more.

music

  • - elsewhere is a normal venue
  • - mercury lounge
  • - nowadays books a lot of good stuff
  • - bossa nova civic club - fun on weekdays sometimes too
  • - wonderville - bar with like an indie arcade and has a lot of cool shows

art

  • - dia (beacon)
  • - metrograph + film forum are great independent theaters
  • - dream house is a cool space to chill
  • - smilers has some unique curation
  • - no gallery but i can't vouch for it
  • - 56 henry with same comment
  • - the met opera / ballet - try to get rush tickets

museums

  • - the new museum is consistently cool
  • - the met and try to book lunch at cafe sabarsky if you can
  • - moma
  • - don't go to the guggenheim unless something good is going on it kinda sucks
  • - i sort of hate the whitney but sometime you gotta hand it to them

bars

  • - bar goto
  • - split eights
  • - 169 bar has cheap beers last i checked
  • - bemelmans for martinis
  • - sharlene's / the sackett
  • - everyone talks about clandestino so it's probably fine
  • - decibel for sake

to-do

  • - nyc resistor (hackerspace) is cool if you can catch them on an open house night
  • - albertine and then maybe walk around central park
  • - spa 88 for a banya experience, make sure to order food it goes crazy
  • - chess forum if ur into that
